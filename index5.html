    Gudetama can adapt to anything. From your morning coffee cup to your lunch restaurant. The Sanrio Company has teamed up with a restrauant in California, called Curry House, to bring Gudetama into their store menu starting from October 20th, 2017 to January 20th, 2018.


    Their Gudetama meal set includes a bowl of curry rice with Gudetama, a Gudetama salad, a Gudetama pudding and a Gudetama cream soup. This is the cartoon version of their meal...

    ... and here is their actual meal set.

    Customers also recieve a limited edition Gudetama beanie and a collectible placemats. This meal set only cost $29.
